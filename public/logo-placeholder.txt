Please add your logo image here as "logo.png"

The logo should be:
- A white ghost-like figure with dripping effect
- On a transparent or blue gradient background
- Recommended size: 512x512px or larger (will be scaled down)
- Format: PNG with transparency preferred

The app will automatically use this logo in:
- Sidebar navigation
- Landing page
- Sign in page

If you don't have the logo yet, the app will still work but the logo images won't display.
